Barbeque Nation is a popular Indian restaurant chain known for its buffet-style dining experience centered around barbecued meats and an assortment of dishes from Indian and international cuisines. Founded in 2006, it has rapidly expanded across India and also has outlets in other countries like the UAE, Malaysia, and Oman
